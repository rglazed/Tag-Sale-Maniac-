1. Logline

When a thrifty New England mom snags a box of “mystery collectibles” at a Saturday tag sale, she unleashes an ancient spirit that feeds on second‑hand souls—turning the town’s summer yard‑sale circuit into a blood‑soaked bargain hunt.

2. Elevator Pitch

Think Stranger Things nostalgia meets Evil Dead Rise splatter, wrapped in the suburban satire of The Stepford Wives. Every neon price sticker is a curse stamp, every “$1‑Deal!” sign a sinister trap.

3. Main Characters
